Satellite Defense Toolkit

The Satellite Defense Toolkit is a modular red and blue team framework for space-based and GNSS cybersecurity research and operations.

  • GNSS spoofing and anomaly detection
  • RF jammer localization and signal mapping
  • Telemetry analysis and anomaly monitoring
  • Command-line and dashboard interfaces
  • Modular architecture with full automation capabilities
